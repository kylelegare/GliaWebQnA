ADA ComplianceGlia is constantly working to ensure that anyone with disabilities seeking information or services from us, have access to and use of information and data that is comparable to the access and use by individuals without disabilities, within reason. Below are some of the steps we are taking under our accessibility policy for the customer facing aspects of our platform:Glia employs experienced accessibility developers and experts to conduct a accessibility audit and provide Glia web site developers or 3rd party contractors with specific recommendations to facilitate accessibility and conformance with WCAG 2.1 AA standards.On an ongoing basis, Glia will have periodic accessibility audits, as well as end user accessibility testing and evaluation conducted in order to provide prioritised feedback regarding conformance with WCAG 2.1 AA standards.Glia has adopted this accessibility policy to support its commitment to the accessibility of Glia.com. Glia is committed to facilitating the accessibility and usability of its Web site, and access to electronic and information technology, for all people with disabilities.
